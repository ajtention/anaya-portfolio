My Journey

I'm from Atlanta, GA, but I've been living in Kennesaw while attending Kennesaw State University. I'm a junior majoring in Data Science and Analytics with a minor in IT.

I first got into data science during my sophomore year. At the time, I didn't know much about it, but the more I learned, the more it clicked. I started with basic analytics, then got into machine learning and predictive modeling, and now I really enjoy working with data to find patterns and insights.

For me, data science is all about solving real problems and making sense of things that might not be obvious at first. Whether it's improving a process or uncovering trends, I like knowing that my work can actually make an impact.
